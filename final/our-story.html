Our Humble Beginnings

Pacific Trails Resort started as a small family-run retreat tucked along the breathtaking Big Sur coastline. From the very beginning, our goal has been to create a place where guests can truly disconnect, embrace nature, and experience the wonders of the Pacific in their purest form.

Our Vision and Values

We believe that a great getaway begins with authenticity, comfort, and care for the environment. Every decision we make is guided by a commitment to sustainability, community, and exceptional hospitality. From designing our cozy yurts to preserving the surrounding trails, we aim to preserve the incredible environment that surrounds and sustains us.

Growing Into a Jewel of Big Sur

Over the years, our retreat has evolved into a premier destination for travelers seeking adventure, relaxation, and unforgettable coastal views. What has not changed is our dedication to personal service and creating a welcoming atmosphere for every guest.

Our Commitment to You

Whether you’re hiking through coastal trails, enjoying a sunset on the cliffs, kayaking in the deep blue, or curling up in one of our yurts, our mission is simple: to offer a retreat that rejuvenates the body, inspires the mind, and touches the heart. We invite you to be part of our ongoing story.
